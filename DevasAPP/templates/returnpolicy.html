Our Commitment

Cancellation & Replacement Policy

Due to the perishable nature of plants, returns are generally not accepted. However, if the plant arrives damaged, you may contact us with opening video of the damaged product, and we will offer a replacement If there is damage to the plants in the courier which you pick up correctly within the time period specified by us, you can inform to our official mail id along with the opening video. There will be no replacement for orders received late,If you wish to cancel an order, please send a cancellation request to our official email, mentioning your Order ID. If the order has not been dispatched, we will process the cancellation and initiate a refund within 24 working hours

Damaged on Arrival?

Plants are delicate travelers. If your plant arrives with significant structural damage, broken stems, or severe wilting, we will make it right.

Replacement Only

To keep our garden growing, we offer replacements only for damaged items. We do not issue monetary refunds once a plant has been shipped.

48-Hour Guarantee

Please inspect your green friends immediately. Claims must be submitted within 48 hours of delivery to qualify for a replacement.

How to request a replacement after product received:

  • 01. Take opening video of the damaged plant and the original packaging.
  • 02. Go to Track Your Order Page, There you can see the deliverded product with a option to Return. Click and fill out the form.
  • 03. Our botanists will review the claim and ship your new plant within 5-7 business days.

Disclaimer: Minor foliage shedding or small leaf tears are natural occurrences during transit and are not considered "damage." Our policy does not cover plants that fail to thrive due to improper care, overwatering, or insufficient light conditions after arrival.
