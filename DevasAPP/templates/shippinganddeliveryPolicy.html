Shipping and Delivery Policy

Please read these Shipping and Delivery Policy carefully before using Deva's Garden website or services.

1. Introduction

We ship live plants and take all necessary precautions to ensure they arrive in healthy condition. Plants are typically shipped within [10 to 15 working days] after an order is placed. Once the plant is dispatched, we will provide you with a tracking ID

2. Delivery Instructions

You are required to collect the plant within 2 days after receiving the tracking ID to avoid damage due to delays. If the plant is not collected within this time, we will not be responsible for any damage that may occur.

3. Customer Responsibilities

Once the plant is delivered, it is the customer’s responsibility to care for it according to the instructions provided. We are not liable for plant health after delivery unless it is proven that the plant was damaged during transit.

4. Tracking and Communication

We will provide you with the tracking details via the registered Email .It is your responsibility to track the order and ensure timely collection. If you do not collect the order within the specified time, we cannot be held liable for the condition of the plant
